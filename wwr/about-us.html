About Us

Boy Scouts often have the opertunity to camp, but not all get to go rafting. Two young Boy Scouts from Colorado got this oportunity, and it inspired them to create Raftin. Now just like them, you too can experience rafting!

Our Mission

Our mission is to help everyone learn to enjoy nature as we do. In an era of cell phones and cars, not too many people get out to enjoy nature any more, and we find that sad. Because of that, we made it our mission to make adventures in the outdoors easy and accessible to all! We book rafting experiences for all kinds of people, those who have never gone rafting before, for those who want something a little more extreme, and for those who need something a little gentler. We make it our duty to make rafting easy and accessible to that everyone can experience the wonders of nature!
